Hey! I'm NVitschDEV — a developer who blends technical craftsmanship with creative expression. My toolkit spans terminal utilities in Python, game development in Godot, 3D modeling, and cinematic video editing with DaVinci Resolve.

I'm a proud Arch Linux user (yes, I use Arch btw) and enjoy building tools that actually solve problems — like ptodo, a terminal todo-app that made it into the AUR.
